Issues Around Timekeeping and Timestamps.

Existing time synchronization protocols like NTP, TAI, UTC, GPS, and timestamp protocols such as ISO 8601 and RFC 3339, despite their widespread use, are susceptible to manipulation and inaccuracies due to their reliance on centralized sources, the inability to verify the data and a lack of a unified global standard. This discrepancy poses significant risks in sectors where precise timekeeping and time-stamping is essential.

01 · What is Clockchain?

A new type of blockchain clock.

A new type of blockchain clock, setting a new standard of global timekeeping and timestamping. A verifiable and immutable version of time, Clockchain serves critical sectors like finance, supply chain, and autonomous systems.

With its decentralized structure and token economy the Clockchain network is a democratic solution for the digital age, defining the future of time.

02 · Vision

A foundational pillar of Web3 infrastructure.

Our vision is to secure and revolutionize the integrity of time-related data through blockchain technology, becoming a foundational pillar of Web3 infrastructure. We aim to enable autonomous systems to operate with enhanced accountability, transparency, and trust.

03 · Mission

A new global standard for time.

The Clockchain is a time focused blockchain network. Its mission is to create a new global standard for accuracy and security in timekeeping and timestamping, and integrating these innovations into forthcoming Web 3.0 applications.

The solution.

Three primitives, working together. A decentralized physical network of certified time sources, aggregated by AI, validated on-chain through Proof-of-Time consensus.

01

Decentralized timestamping via DePIN.

A physical infrastructure network of global certification authorities — Safran atomic clocks, NICT, GPS, atomic time sources — manages timestamping across regions for accuracy that no single authority can tamper with.

DePIN · Distributed sources
02

AI + blockchain integration.

AI models reconcile time data across heterogeneous sources, weighting and ranking inputs in real time, then propagating the validated state to nodes worldwide for instant consensus.

Adaptive consensus · Sub-second
03

Proof-of-Time consensus.

A new consensus mechanism that aggregates time data from all global sources and validates it on-chain — ensuring accuracy and consistency across every block, every second.

PoT · On-chain validation
